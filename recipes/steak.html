Jalapeno Steak Recipe

Description

This should be one delicious steak. If you do not cook it medium rare, don't cook it at all

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Jalapeno
  • Lots of pepper

Steps

  1. Season the steak
  2. Place it on grill for 5 minutes and flip over
  3. Cook it for another 5 minutes and let it rest
